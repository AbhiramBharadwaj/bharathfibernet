Keep Your IT Infrastructure at Peak Performance

Your business depends on reliable IT systems. At Bharath VoIP, our Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) are designed to ensure the long-term health and reliability of your hardware, software, and networking assets—without breaking your budget. From routine maintenance to rapid issue resolution, we keep your systems running at peak efficiency with minimal downtime.

Scope of Services

  • Comprehensive Hardware & Network Support: Proactive care for all your IT equipment.
  • Periodic Health Checks & Preventive Maintenance: Identify and fix issues before they affect your business.
  • On-Site & Remote Support: Flexible support options tailored to your business operations.
  • Replacement of Faulty Equipment (T&C Apply): Quick turnaround to keep your systems operational.
  • Customized AMC Plans: Scalable contracts designed around your specific needs.

Devices Covered

Our AMC covers a wide range of devices critical to your business operations:

  • Routers & Firewalls
  • Switches & Servers
  • Workstations & Desktops
  • Enterprise Laptops & Devices
  • Surveillance Systems & Security Hardware
